The Educational Labeling System @Atmospheres (ELSA) is a tool designed to help streamline the creation of PDS4 compliant labels. Here at Atmos, we believe in taking a top-down approach when it comes to your data. ELSA begins with the unique name of your bundle, trickles down through your collections, and ends with the details of your data to provide you with PDS4 labels.
